Potato State Rivalry Roadtrip

2 weekends ago I had the opportunity to go to the Potato State Rivalry game against Idaho State University and the University of Idaho.
I went on this trip because me and some friends attended Idaho State Univesity and we have not seen a Bengal win since before we were in school.

On 30 hour round trip that we took, we drove through many cities.

  1. Salt Lake City
  2. Pocatello
  3. Idaho Falls
  4. Butte
  5. Missoula
  6. CDA
  7. Moscow
  8. Lewiston
  9. Boise
We also did multiple things before the game
  • Tailgate
  • Facility Tour
  • Throw the football

While at the game I got to see my friends play football and beat the Vandals in Moscow for the first time since 1981. My friend Jordan Cooke had 4 touchdowns and dominated the vandal defense.
