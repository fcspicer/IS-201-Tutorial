Potato State Rivalry Roadtrip

30-hour journey, legendary matchups, and an unforgettable win in Moscow.

Two weekends ago I had the chance to travel for the Potato State Rivalry game between Idaho State University and the University of Idaho. A few of my friends played at Idaho State, and we had not seen a Bengal win since before we were students.

The 30-hour round trip packed in big moments, surprising stops, and a victory we will never forget.

Roadtrip Route

  1. Salt Lake City
  2. Pocatello
  3. Idaho Falls
  4. Butte
  5. Missoula
  6. CDA
  7. Moscow
  8. Lewiston
  9. Boise

Game Day Highlights

  • Tailgate with friends
  • Facility tour
  • Throwing passes on the field

At the game, I watched my friends beat the Vandals in Moscow for the first time since 1981. Jordan Cooke scored four touchdowns and led a dominant offensive performance.
